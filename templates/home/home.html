What We Offer

Everything you need to feel a little better

Small, gentle tools designed to support your mental wellness — one check-in at a time.

😊

Mood Check-Ins

A simple emoji-based daily check-in. No pressure, no lengthy forms — just a moment to acknowledge how you're feeling today.

🤖

AI-Powered Insights

Gemini AI gently analyzes your patterns over time — spotting signs of stress, burnout, or anxiety before they overwhelm you.

🌿

Personalized Coping

Get tailored suggestions — breathing exercises, calming music, study break reminders, or a gentle walk nudge — based on your mood.

📓

Optional Journaling

A private, judgment-free space to write down your thoughts. Your words stay yours — encrypted, private, and never shared. Sometimes putting it into words is all you need.

📈

Weekly Wellness Trends

Beautiful, simple graphs showing your emotional journey across the week — so you can see your own progress and patterns.

Simple Process

Four small steps.
A meaningful difference.

No complicated setup. No overwhelming forms. Just a gentle routine that fits into your day.

01

Daily Check-In

Tap an emoji that matches how you're feeling. Takes under 30 seconds — no overthinking needed.

02

Journal (Optional)

Write a few words about your day if you'd like. Totally optional, completely private.

03

AI Reads the Pattern

Gemini AI quietly notices trends across your check-ins — gently flagging stress patterns you might not notice yourself.

04

Get Gentle Suggestions

Receive personalised coping recommendations — small, doable actions that actually help.
